HAVIT HV-KB390L

Colors available: Black

The HAVIT HV-KB390L is a low-profile mechanical keyboard. Because of the short travel distance, the "clack" noise of bottoming out is much quieter than other clicky keyboards. Former users of chiclet keyboards would find the HAVIT HV-KB390L easy to transition into.

Technical specifications

  • Switch: Kailh PG1350 Blue
  • Anti-ghosting: Yes
  • Backlighting: LED (Ice Blue)
  • Color: Black
  • Form factor: Tenkeyless
  • Keycap material: ABS
  • Macro support: Yes
  • USB key rollover: Full
