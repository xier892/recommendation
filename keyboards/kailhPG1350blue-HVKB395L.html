HAVIT HV-KB395L

Colors available: Black

The HAVIT HV-KB395L is a low-profile mechanical keyboard. Because of the short travel distance, the "clack" noise of bottoming out is much quieter than other clicky keyboards. Former users of chiclet keyboards would find the HAVIT HV-KB395L easy to transition into.
